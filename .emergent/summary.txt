<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a factory management tool with the following modules:
1.  **Raw Material (RM) Management**: Bulk upload RM IDs, enter daily purchase quantities, and track inventory.
2.  **SKU Management**: Create and manage finished goods SKU IDs.
3.  **BOM Mapping**: Map RM IDs to SKU IDs, specifying the quantity of each RM required per SKU.
4.  **Production Entry**: Record production counts against SKU IDs.
5.  **Inventory Consumption**: Automatically deduct RM inventory based on production entries.
6.  **Dispatch Tracking**: Mark outgoing SKUs as dispatched.
7.  **Reporting**: Provide a net-off tracking of RM and SKU inventory.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Based on subsequent requests):**
- **Multi-Branch Architecture**: The system must support 7 specific branches. Users select a branch after logging in.
- **Role-Based Access Control**:
    - **Master Admin**: Global view across all branches, can manage users.
    - **Branch User**: View and edit data only for their assigned branch.
- **Global IDs**: SKUs and RMs are created globally with sequential IDs and then activated for use in specific branches.
- **Detailed SKU/RM Fields**: SKUs and RMs have specific, detailed fields. RM IDs are auto-generated based on their category.
- **Bulk Operations**: The system must support bulk uploading of SKUs from an Excel file and SKU-RM mappings.
- **Production Planning**: A module to upload a monthly production plan and get a shortage analysis for raw materials at the branch level.
- **RM Inward**: A dedicated entry point to record the receipt of raw materials at a branch.

**User's preferred language**: English
